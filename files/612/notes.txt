## Hypothesis on root cause
The h2_end_stream=1 atomic 2-frame DATA strategy may have changed the connection-close signature from 'ERR_CONNECTION_CLOSED' (abrupt RST/FIN before headers) to '(canceled)' (graceful close at HTTP level but no response body delivered). Even with atomic delivery, the engine appears to be producing a response that the client either:
  (a) never receives any HTTP response (Response headers = 0), or
  (b) receives but JS treats as invalid SSE and aborts via fetch.signal.abort()

Either way, the SSE event stream did not deliver any events the frontend could parse into the '보안 경고' warning text. The atomic delivery hypothesis (eliminate ERR_CONNECTION_CLOSED) is partially confirmed — the explicit ERR_CONNECTION_CLOSED disappeared — but the user-visible outcome (no warning render) remains identical to #607.
