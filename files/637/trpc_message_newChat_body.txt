## Diagnosis
The HTTP layer returned 200 OK in 81 ms (way too fast for a real LLM call → MITM intercept fired).
But the body shape did NOT match what the tRPC client expects. tRPC client tried `Object.entries(<something>)` on a property that was undefined and threw immediately.

The native (un-intercepted) success body for tRPC v10 batch=1 must look like:
  [{"result":{"data":{"json":{ ... actual newChat payload ... }, "meta":{...}}}}]
or for a tRPC error:
  [{"error":{"json":{"message":"...","code":-32000,"data":{"code":"BAD_REQUEST","httpStatus":400,...}}}}]

Whatever the engine returned has a missing/undefined sub-object that `Object.entries` chokes on (likely missing `meta` or missing `result.data.json` envelope, or missing `data` inside `error`).
